Zasady ochrany osobnych udajov

Tieto zasady ochrany osobnych udajov sa vztahuju na aplikaciu Regnum Christi Slovensko (dalej len „aplikacia“) pre mobilne zariadenia, ktoru vyvinulo Obcianske zdruzenie RCSK (dalej len „poskytovatel sluzby“). Tato sluzba je poskytovana „TAK, AKO JE“ ako bezplatna, nekomercna sluzba.

Identifikacia prevadzkovatela

Prevadzkovatelom osobnych udajov je Obcianske zdruzenie RCSK, so sidlom Ludovita Fullu 20, 841 05 Bratislava-Karlova Ves, ICO: 53163966.

Ake informacie aplikacia ziskava a ako ich pouziva?

Aplikacia ziskava informacie, ktore poskytnete pri stiahnutí a pouzivani aplikacie. Registracia u poskytovatela sluzby nie je povinna na pouzivanie zakladnych funkcii (Spravy, Modlitebnik, Kalendar, Galeria).

V zavislosti od pouzivanych funkcii vsak aplikacia spracuva konkretne udaje:

  • Udaje z kontaktneho formulara: Ak pouzijete kontaktny formular v aplikacii, uvediete svoje meno, e-mailovu adresu a spravu. Tieto udaje su bezpecne odovzdane a ulozene v databaze poskytovatela sluzby, aby sme mohli odpovedat na vasu ziadost.
  • Tokeny push notifikacii: Ak sa rozhodnete prijimat vzdialene aktualizacie sprav („Upozornenia na novinky“), aplikacia vygeneruje token push notifikacie cez Expo, ktory sa synchronizuje s nasou databazou na dorucenie hromadnych sprav. Tuto moznost mozete kedykolvek vypnut, co aktivne vymaze token z nasich serverov.
  • Lokalne udaje v zariadeni: Vase vlastne modlitby, oblubene modlitby a nastavenia aplikacie (napr. velkost textu a tema) sa ukladaju vyhradne lokalne vo vasom zariadeni (AsyncStorage). Tieto udaje sa nikdy neodosielajú poskytovatelovi sluzby.

Poskytovatel sluzby moze pouzit informacie, ktore ste poskytli, na kontakt s vami s cielom poskytnut dolezite informacie, povinne oznamenia alebo odpovedat na vase poziadavky podpory.

Ziskava aplikacia informacie automaticky?

Aplikacia moze automaticky ziskavat urcite minimalne informacie, vratane typu mobilneho zariadenia, ktore pouzivate, vasho mobilneho operacneho systemu a zaznamov o padoch aplikacie (crash logs), aby nam pomohli zlepsit stabilitu.

Ziskava aplikacia presne informacie o polohe zariadenia v realnom case?

Nie. Tato aplikacia nezhromazduje presne informacie o polohe vasho mobilneho zariadenia.

Pouziva aplikacia technologie umelej inteligencie (AI)?

Nie. Aplikacia nepouziva technologie umelej inteligencie (AI) na spracuvanie vasich udajov ani na poskytovanie funkcii.

Maju tretie strany pristup k informaciam ziskanym aplikaciou?

Vase informacie zdielame s tretimi stranami iba sposobmi uvedenymi v tomto vyhlaseni o ochrane osobnych udajov. Aplikacia vyuziva konkretnych poskytovatelov infrastruktury tretich stran, ktori vystupuju ako sprostredkovatelia spracuvania:

  • Expo: Umoznuje funkcnost aplikacie a dorucovanie push notifikacii.
  • Supabase: Sluzi ako nasa bezpecna backendova databaza pre spravy z kontaktneho formulara a tokeny push notifikacii.
  • Google Calendar / Cloudflare / WordPress: Pouzivaju sa vyhradne na nacitanie verejnych udajov len na citanie (udalosti, obrazky a clanky) zobrazenych v aplikacii.

Poskytovatel sluzby moze spristupnit informacie poskytnute pouzivatelmi a informacie ziskane automaticky:

  • ak to vyzaduje zakon, napriklad na splnenie povinnosti voci sudnemu predvolaniu alebo podobnemu pravnemu procesu;
  • ked v dobrej viere usudime, ze spristupnenie je nevyhnutne na ochranu nasich prav, ochranu vasej bezpecnosti alebo bezpecnosti inych, vysetrovanie podvodov alebo odpoved na ziadost statnych organov;
  • nasim doveryhodnym poskytovatelom sluzieb, ktori pracuju v nasom mene, nemaju samostatne vyuzitie informacii, ktore im spristupnime, a suhlasili s dodrziavanim pravidiel uvedenych v tomto vyhlaseni o ochrane osobnych udajov.

Ake mam pravo na odhlasenie?

Zber vsetkych informacii aplikaciou mozete jednoducho zastavit odinštalovanim aplikacie standardnym postupom pre vase zariadenie. Mozete tiez okamzite zrusit suhlas s push notifikaciami ich vypnutim na karte „Nastavenia“, co natrvalo vymaze vas token z nasich serverov.

Aka je politika uchovavania udajov a ako mozete spravovat svoje informacie?

Osobne udaje uchovavame len po dobu nevyhnutnu na splnenie ucelov opisanych v tychto zasadach ochrany osobnych udajov. Konkretne:

  • Udaje z kontaktneho formulara: zvycajne nie dlhsie ako 12 mesiacov po vybaveni vasej ziadosti.
  • Technicke zaznamy (vratane zaznamov o padoch aplikacie a suvisiacich logov): najviac 30 dni na ucely bezpecnosti a odstranovania chyb.
  • Tokeny push notifikacii: len pocas toho, ako zostavate prihlaseni na ich prijem; po vypnuti upozorneni v aplikacii sa token z nasich serverov vymaze.

Ak chcete, aby poskytovatel sluzby vymazal akekolvek pouzivatelom poskytnute udaje, ktore ste odoslali prostrednictvom aplikacie, kontaktujte nas na info@regnumchristi.sk a odpovieme v primeranej lehote.

Sulad s GDPR a vase prava na ochranu osobnych udajov (Zakon c. 18/2018 Z. z.)

Kedze poskytovatel sluzby posobi na Slovensku, vase udaje su chranene vseobecnym nariadenim o ochrane udajov (GDPR) a zakonom c. 18/2018 Z. z. o ochrane osobnych udajov v zneni neskorsich predpisov. Mate pravo na:

  • Pristup: Poziadat o kopiu vasich osobnych udajov.
  • Opravu: Poziadat o opravu nepresnych osobnych udajov.
  • Vymazanie: Poziadat o vymazanie vasich osobnych udajov („pravo byt zabudnuty“).
  • Obmedzenie a namietku: Obmedzit alebo namietat proti sposobu, akym spracuvame vase udaje.

Na uplatnenie tychto prav napiste na info@regnumchristi.sk. Mate tiez pravo podat staznost na Urad na ochranu osobnych udajov SR.

Ako aplikacia pristupuje k ochrane osobnych udajov mladsich pouzivatelov?

Aplikacia Regnum Christi Slovensko je urcena pre cele spolocenstvo Regnum Christi na Slovensku, vratane deti a mladych ludi. Funkcie ako Modlitebnik, pripomienky udalosti v kalendari a Galeria su zamerne pristupne aj mladsim clenom nasho spolocenstva.

Aplikacia zhromazduje minimalne osobne udaje (pozri vyssie). Poskytovatel sluzby nezhromazduje vedome osobne udaje specificky od deti. Kontaktny formular a suhlas s push notifikaciami su jedine funkcie, ktore odosielajú udaje na nase servery; akekolvek osobne udaje zadane prostrednictvom aplikacie by mali byt poskytnute so vedomim rodica alebo zakonneho zastupcu tam, kde je to na mieste.

Bezpecnost a blaho vsetkych clenov spolocenstva, vratane maloletych, upravuje nasa Politika ochrany (Safeguarding Policy), ktora je dostupna priamo v aplikacii v sekcii FAQ. Odporucame vsetkym rodicom a zakonnym zastupcom, aby si ju precitali.

Ak ma rodic alebo zakonny zastupca obavy o spracuvanie udajov svojho dietata, kontaktujte nas na info@regnumchristi.sk.

Ako su vase informacie zabezpecene?

Poskytovatel sluzby zabezpecuje fyzicke, elektronicke a procesne zaruky (vratane Row Level Security v nasich databazach a sifrovanych pripojeni API) na ochranu informacii, ktore spracuvame a uchovavame. Pristup k tymto informaciam obmedzujeme vyhradne na opravneny personal, ktory ich potrebuje na prevadzku alebo zlepsovanie aplikacie.

Ako budete informovani o zmenach tychto zasad ochrany osobnych udajov?

Tieto zasady ochrany osobnych udajov mozu byt z casu na cas aktualizovane. Poskytovatel sluzby vas o zmenach informuje aktualizaciou tejto stranky. Odporucame vam pravidelne si tieto zasady ochrany osobnych udajov precitat kvoli pripadnym zmenam.

Privacy Policy

This privacy policy is applicable to the Regnum Christi Slovensko app (hereinafter referred to as the "Application") for mobile devices, which was developed by Obcianske zdruzenie RCSK (hereinafter referred to as the "Service Provider"). This service is provided "AS IS" as a free, non-commercial service.

Identification of the data controller

The controller of your personal data is Obcianske zdruzenie RCSK, with its registered office at Ludovita Fullu 20, 841 05 Bratislava-Karlova Ves, Slovakia, identification number (ICO): 53163966.

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

The Application acquires the information you supply when you download and use the Application. Registration with the Service Provider is not mandatory to use the core features (News, Prayerbook, Calendar, Gallery).

However, depending on the features you use, the Application processes specific data:

  • Contact form data: If you use the in-app contact form, you provide your name, email address, and message. This data is securely transmitted to and stored in the Service Provider's database so we can respond to your inquiry.
  • Push notification tokens: If you opt in to receive remote news updates ("Upozornenia na novinky"), the Application generates a push notification token via Expo, which is synced to our database to deliver broadcast messages. You can toggle this off at any time, which actively deletes the token from our servers.
  • Local on-device data: Your custom prayers, favorite prayers, and app settings (such as text size and theme) are saved strictly locally on your device (AsyncStorage). This data is never transmitted to the Service Provider.

The Service Provider may use the information you provided to contact you to provide important information, required notices, or respond to your support requests.

What information does the Application collect automatically?

The Application may collect certain minimal information automatically, including the type of mobile device you use, your mobile operating system, and crash logs to help us improve stability.

Does the Application collect precise real-time location information of the device?

No. This Application does not gather precise information about the location of your mobile device.

Does the Application use Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies?

No. The Application does not use Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies to process your data or provide features.

Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Application?

We only share your information with third parties in the ways described in this privacy statement. The Application utilizes specific third-party infrastructure providers that act as data processors:

  • Expo: Facilitates app functionality and push notification delivery.
  • Supabase: Acts as our secure backend database for contact messages and push notification tokens.
  • Google Calendar / Cloudflare / WordPress: Used solely to fetch public read-only data (events, images, and news articles) displayed in the app.

The Service Provider may disclose user-provided and automatically collected information:

  • as required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena or similar legal process;
  • when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request;
  • with our trusted service providers who work on our behalf, do not have an independent use of the information we disclose to them, and have agreed to adhere to the rules set forth in this privacy statement.

What are my opt-out rights?

You can halt all collection of information by the Application easily by uninstalling the Application using the standard uninstall processes for your device. You can also instantly opt out of push notifications by disabling them in the "Settings" tab, which permanently deletes your token from our servers.

What is the data retention policy and how can you manage your information?

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy. Specifically:

  • Contact form data is typically retained for no longer than 12 months after the inquiry is resolved.
  • Technical records (including crash logs and related server logs) may be retained for up to 30 days for security and debugging purposes.
  • Push notification tokens are retained only while you remain opted in to receive them; when you disable notifications in the app, your token is deleted from our servers.

If you would like the Service Provider to delete any user-provided data you have submitted via the Application, please contact us at info@regnumchristi.sk and we will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

GDPR compliance and your privacy rights (Act No. 18/2018 Coll.)

Because the Service Provider operates in Slovakia, your data is protected under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Slovak Act No. 18/2018 Coll. on the Protection of Personal Data. You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Erasure: Request the deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Restriction & objection: Limit or object to how we process your data.

To exercise these rights, email info@regnumchristi.sk. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Office for Personal Data Protection of the Slovak Republic (Urad na ochranu osobnych udajov SR).

How does the Application address the privacy of younger users?

The Regnum Christi Slovensko app is designed for the entire Regnum Christi community in Slovakia, including children and young people. Features such as the Prayerbook, Calendar event reminders, and the Gallery are intentionally accessible to younger members of our community.

The Application collects minimal personal data (see above). The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personal data specifically from children. The contact form and push notification opt-in are the only features that transmit personal data to our servers; any personal data submitted through the Application should be provided with a parent or legal guardian's awareness where applicable.

The safety and well-being of all community members, including minors, is governed by our Safeguarding Policy, which is available directly within the app in the FAQ section. We encourage all parents and guardians to read it.

If a parent or guardian has any concerns about how their child's data is handled, please contact us at info@regnumchristi.sk.

How is your information kept secure?

The Service Provider provides physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards (including Row Level Security on our databases and encrypted API connections) to protect information we process and maintain. We limit access to this information strictly to authorized personnel who need to know it to operate or improve the Application.

How will you be informed of changes to this Privacy Policy?

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The Service Provider will notify you of any changes by updating this page. You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes.

How can you contact us?
